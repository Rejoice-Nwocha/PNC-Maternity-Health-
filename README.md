## The Problem

Maternal death remains a **leading cause of death for women of reproductive age globally**. The postnatal period â€” the weeks immediately after birth â€” is the most vulnerable window for new mothers. Yet in many parts of the world, this critical window is consistently overlooked.

Key findings from the data:
